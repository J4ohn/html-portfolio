Best Movies 2024 According to JO

Top three Ranging movies

Movie titles and description

Inside Out2

The sequel to Pixar's 2015 hit continues the emotional journey of Riley, now a teenager, exploring new facets of her inner world. The film has grossed approximately $1.7 billion worldwide, making it the highest-grossing Pixar film to date

Deadpool & Wolverine

This crossover film brings together two iconic Marvel characters, Deadpool and Wolverine, in an action-packed adventure. It has earned around $1.3 billion globally, setting a new record for the highest-grossing R-rated Disney film.

Moana 2

The sequel to the beloved 2016 animated film follows Moana on a new voyage across the Pacific. It has amassed over $1 billion worldwide, continuing the story's success.
